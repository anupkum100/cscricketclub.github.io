    About Us

    Welcome to Chhatrapati Shivaji Cricket Club – Where Passion Meets the Pitch

    At Chhatrapati Shivaji Cricket Club, we believe that cricket is more than just a game—it's an emotion that unites communities, shapes character, and inspires generations. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a space where every cricket lover—whether a weekend warrior, a rising star, or a seasoned player—feels at home the moment they step onto the field.

    Fueled by an unwavering love for the game, our ground has been thoughtfully designed to meet the evolving needs of today’s cricketing community. Whether it's a young talent taking their first swing, a coach guiding the next generation, or fans cheering from the sidelines, our venue promises an inclusive, welcoming, and invigorating experience for everyone.

    Strategically located in Pune, our facility is not just a ground—it's a hub of opportunity, ambition, and sporting excellence. We’ve paid attention to every detail—be it pitch quality, safety measures, or off-field amenities—so that the only thing players need to focus on is their performance. With professional-grade infrastructure and a strong sense of community, we aim to set a new standard for cricketing venues in the region.

    Services

    What We Offer

    Top-Class Turf and Matting Pitches

    Our ground features meticulously prepared turf and synthetic pitches, catering to both traditional formats and fast-paced tennis-ball matches.

    Professional Amenities

    From well-maintained outfields and boundary ropes to modern scoreboards and dugouts, every aspect is built to deliver a professional match-day experience.

    Practice Nets & Coaching Zones

    With dedicated practice nets and coaching areas, we aim to support skill development, training sessions, and youth academies.

    Night Matches Ready

    Equipped with high-quality floodlights, we offer the thrill of night cricket—perfect for leagues, after-work games, and tournaments.

    Player-Friendly Facilities

    Our setup includes changing rooms, hydration stations, clean restrooms, seating zones, and a planned pavilion to enhance comfort and convenience.

    Event and League Hosting

    Whether it’s a weekend tournament, a corporate cup, or an academy event, our ground is available for full-day or hourly bookings with flexible packages.

    Our Commitment

    We’re committed to maintaining the highest standards of pitch quality, player safety, and customer satisfaction. Our team consists of ground experts, cricket lovers, and experienced organizers who ensure that every match runs smoothly.
